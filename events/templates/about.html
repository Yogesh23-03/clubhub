About the College

Sir M. Visvesvaraya Institute of Technology (Sir MVIT), established in 1986 under the Sri Krishnadevaraya Educational Trust, is a premier engineering institution located in Bengaluru, Karnataka, offering undergraduate and postgraduate programmes affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU) and approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). Spread over a spacious campus of 133 acres at Krishnadevaraya Nagar, Hunasamaranahalli, North Bengaluru, Sir MVIT blends rigorous academics with holistic development. sirmvit.edu +1 With a focus on nurturing technical competency alongside ethical values, innovation and social responsibility, the institute emphasises state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, industry-linked curriculum and vibrant student life. Sir MVIT’s mission is to empower future-ready engineers through a balanced blend of theory, hands-on practice, research opportunities and co-curricular enrichment, thereby playing a key role in shaping the technology leaders of tomorrow.
